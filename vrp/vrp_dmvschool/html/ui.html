Welcome to the DMV Center


We require all of our citizens to go through a theory test before obtaining a valid driving license.
Take your time, and answer with common sence, do not take any lucky guesses.

Theory Test
- The written test costs $200 bucks, there are no refunds if you fail.
- Do not worry, the DMV Center will allow loans, watch out not to fall in big debt!
- If you fail the test once, you cannot take it again right away, come back another day and try again.

Driving Test
- The Driving test costs $500 bucks, like before, this payment is not refunded if you fail the test.
- The main purpose of the driving test is the attention to detail, pay attention to your surroundings and do not crash!
